Our history

Longest standing team in F1 history

Scuderia Ferrari is the oldest and most successful team in Formula 1 history. Founded by Enzo Ferrari, the team made its debut in the very first Formula 1 season in 1950. Since then, Ferrari has competed continuously in the sport.

What is Ferrari famous for?

Ferrari is famous for its passion, iconic red color known as Rosso Corsa, and its massive global fanbase, the “Tifosi.” The team is based in Maranello, Italy, where all Ferrari cars are designed and built.

Driver History

Over the years, Ferrari has had many legendary drivers, including Michael Schumacher, Niki Lauda, Sebastian Vettel, and Charles Leclerc. The Schumacher era (2000–2004) was especially successful, with five consecutive World Championships.

Championships

Ferrari holds the record for the most Constructors’ Championships and race victories in Formula 1. Although the team has not won a championship in recent years, Ferrari remains a title contender and one of the most iconic teams in motorsport.
